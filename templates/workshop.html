Guide to the Workshop

Workshop Schedule

1. Introduction to Databases

2. Command Line (Code-Along)

3. Command Line Lab (Around the World)

4. Databases (Code-Along)

5. Databases (do it yourself !!!)

To get ready for the workshop...

1. Sign up for an account on Github if you do not have one at https://github.com/

2. Find the labs on Github! These labs were written for beginners by Google Engineers! https://github.com/google/cssi-labs

3. Click on Repositories on the navigation bar on top!

4. Click on databases on the navigation bar on top!

5. Click on the green button that says "Clone or download" on the right!

6. Click "Download Zip"

7. Open "Atom" application

8. Once in Atom, go to "File" ---> "Open..." and find the zip called "cssi-labs" you downloaded, and then find "command-line"----> "labs"---->, and then finally open "around-the-world"... make sure you get the whole folder!

9. After the first lab is done... Once in Atom, go to "File" ---> "Open..." and find the zip called "cssi-labs" you downloaded, and then find "appEngine-Datastore"----> and open "app-engine-starter"... make sure you get the whole folder!
